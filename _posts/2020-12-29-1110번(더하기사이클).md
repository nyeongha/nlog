### 백준-1110번(더하기사이클)-python

```python
def hap(x,y):
    return x+y
i=int(input())

c = [[0 for j in range(2)] for i in range(i)]
d=[]
for a,b in c:
    a,b=map(int,input().split())
    d.append(hap(a,b))
for i in d:
    print(i)
```
